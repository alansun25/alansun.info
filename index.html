projects & contributions

Formulacademy

Formulacademy is a free library of formulas and online calculators. Its purpose is to save time by eliminating the need for repetitive typing into handheld calculators as well as providing a centralized, convenient location for students to find useful formulas in several different subjects (math, chemistry, physics, economics). I led the idea and development process for this project. Created during VandyHacks 2020 - Summer Edition.

Spendlab

Spendlab is a visual budgeting tool made for the grant-funded community. Its interface uses easy-to-interpret graphs and trendlines to create an intuitive way of tracking grants and lab expenses. I'm currently interning with Spendlab, and my primary role is to conduct UX research through usability tests and interviews. I'm also helping the developer design and create a new feature that will provide the software with another useful functionality.

Rebuild Our Shops

Since the Black Lives Matter protests began in the summer of 2020, many businesses have been looted and destroyed. Rebuild Our Shops absolutely stands with the Black Lives Matter movement. We also wish to support the small businesses that have been affected by riots. On our website, we list locally-owned businesses, bring attention to their fundraising efforts, and give users a way to find and support businesses they've otherwise never heard of before. I helped with frontend development.
